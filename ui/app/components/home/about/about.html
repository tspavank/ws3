Welcome to the ZERV Marketplace



What is the ZERV marketplace ?

ZERV marketplace is a platform to transact with businesses across the ammunition supply chain.The mission is to connect buyers and sellers in a frictionless and integrated fashion to facilitate relationships and end to end commerce activities across the globe.

What can i do here ?


Sell products

With the option to list specific inventory or general selling interests you will increase sales due to presence here

Buy products

Set up alerts so that you will know when all your favorite items are for sale.No more endless searches - the matches will come to you!

Negotiate with trading partners

With the ability to easily negotiate on specific terms or products you'll always get the best deal

Manage and track orders

Keep tabs on your sales and orders to ensure that you always know where your products are

Trace the supply chain

You can track the entire supply chain for every product on the platform.If there's ever any issues you can trace it back to the exact sources and identify what went wrong
